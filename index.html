I am a Ph.D. candidate in CSE department of The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, supervised by Prof. Dit-Yan Yeung. Previously, I graduated with the first class honors in CSE department from The Chinese University of Hong Kong.

My research interests include natural language processing and its applications. Currently, I mainly focus on evaluating and analyzing the abilities of Large Language Models and enhancing them through reinforcement learning training.
